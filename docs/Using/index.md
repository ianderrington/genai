Managing The GenAI amounts to effectively successfully working with the evolving technology in such a way that it creates.

## Strategy for using

1. Identify needs that may be enabled with GenAI, considering the different [examples](examples/index.md)
1. Ensure your efforts do not bend important [ethical](ethically/index.md) considerations of using GenAI. 
1. Identify whether you want to [build, or buy](building_and_buying.md)
1. [Deploy](../Understanding/deploying/index.md) your solution or componentns of it. 
1. Be ready to [manage](managing/index.md)
1. [Mark your, and detect others'](marking_and_detecting.md) AI-generated content.
