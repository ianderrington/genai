# The Honest Majority

I helped to establish [The Honest Majority](https://www.thehonestmajority.org), an initiative that encourages us to shift our perceptions beyond zero-sum outcomes through the intentional use of thoughtful and effective honesty. It focuses on the use of story to share important considerations surrounding truth and how to be effective and thoughtful with it.

## Our Approach

The Honest Majority is built on the belief that:

1. **Most people value honesty** - Despite appearances, most people prefer honest interactions
2. **Honesty can be both thoughtful and effective** - Being honest doesn't mean being blunt or hurtful
3. **Stories are powerful vehicles for truth** - Narrative helps us understand complex ethical considerations

## Storytelling for Truth

The initiative uses storytelling as a primary method for exploring and sharing ideas about honesty because:

- Stories engage our emotions as well as our intellect
- Narratives help us see situations from multiple perspectives
- Characters in stories can model different approaches to honesty
