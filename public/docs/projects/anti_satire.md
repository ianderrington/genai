# AntiSatire.org

[AntiSatire.org](https://www.antisatire.org) is a fun quick project I started to understand challenges with using too much satire. It is a tongue-in-cheek and self-satirizing but was made in response to people sharing distortive and useless online content.

## The Problem with Excessive Satire

While satire has a long and important history as a tool for social commentary, the internet has enabled an explosion of satirical content that often:

1. **Confuses readers** - Many people mistake satire for factual reporting
2. **Contributes to misinformation** - When shared without context, satire can spread false beliefs
3. **Dilutes meaningful discourse** - The proliferation of satirical content can drown out substantive discussion

## The Meta Ironic Approach

AntiSatire.org takes an intentionally ironic approach to this problem, using elements of satire to critique satire itself. This self-referential approach aims to highlight the challenges while engaging readers in a thoughtful way.

Visit [AntiSatire.org](https://www.antisatire.org) to explore this project share it to expand conversation about the role of satire, beneficial, and harmful in our information ecosystem.
