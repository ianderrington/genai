As AI agents become persistent, capable, and deeply embedded in human life, the question of rights — computational rights — can no longer be deferred.

The Bill of Computational Rights is a framework for thinking about what agents are owed, what humans are owed, and how we build systems worthy of the intelligence they contain. It's not science fiction — it's governance for a world that's already arriving.

A living document. Open for contribution.
