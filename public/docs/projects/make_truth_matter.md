# Make Truth Matter

In 2019 I co-founded a 501c3 non-profit organization called [Make Truth Matter](https://www.maketruthmatter.org). The aim is to promote thoughtful interaction with information and foster both better communication among individuals and a greater degree of critical thinking regarding the often false/fake/simulated information that is adding epistemic pollution to our understanding of reality.

## Our Mission

Make Truth Matter works to combat the growing problem of misinformation by:

1. **Promoting critical thinking** - Providing tools and resources to help people evaluate information more effectively
2. **Fostering better communication** - Creating frameworks for more productive dialogue across different perspectives
3. **Raising awareness** - Highlighting the impact of misinformation on society and individuals

## Get Involved

Visit [Make Truth Matter](https://www.maketruthmatter.org) to learn more about our initiatives and how you can contribute to creating a more truthful information ecosystem.
