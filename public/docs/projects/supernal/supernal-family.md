Supernal Family is an educational content site for kids and families. Songs, stories, and lessons — all generated with AI and designed to make complex ideas accessible and fun.

The philosophy: label content by age floor, not age ceiling. A 6-year-old with a curious parent should be able to try a Rocket-level song. We encourage stretch over gatekeeping.

Content includes: actually useful nursery rhymes, Aesop's Fables reimagined, mental model songs, The Fundaments story series, and more.
