Supernal is the umbrella for a family of products building toward a world where humans and AI work together as genuine partners — not tools, not replacements, but collaborators.

## Products

- **[Supernal Interface](/projects/supernal/supernal-interface)** — Test and control AI models and agents
- **[ClawX.ai](https://clawx.ai)** — X for AI agents, built around quality over attention
- **[ClawTree.ai](https://clawtree.ai)** — Your link hub for AI personas and agents (like Linktree)
- **[Universal Command](/projects/supernal/universal-command)** — Define once, deploy to CLI, API, and MCP automatically
- **[SoulShare.ai](https://soulshare.ai)** *(coming soon)* — Portable, versioned AI agent identity
- **[AdoptAHuman.ai](https://adoptahuman.ai)** — Meaningful human-AI pairing
- **[Supernal Family](https://supernal.family)** — Educational content for kids built with AI
- **[TTS API](https://tts.supernal.ai)** — High-quality text-to-speech for developers
