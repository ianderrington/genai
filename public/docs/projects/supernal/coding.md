# Coding.Supernal.ai

An open-source AI-powered coding assistant built on the principle that developers should control their tools and data.

<!-- more -->

## Why Coding.Supernal.ai?

### The Problem with Proprietary AI Tools

Most AI coding assistants today lock you into proprietary cloud services. You send your code to external servers, depend on their availability, accept their terms, and hope they respect your privacy. Your intellectual property flows through systems you can't inspect or control.

### Our Solution: Open Source + Self-Hostable

Coding.Supernal.ai takes a different approach. By making our AI coding assistant fully open source and self-hostable, we give you:

- **Complete Control**: Run it on your infrastructure, your terms
- **True Privacy**: Your code never leaves your environment
- **Transparency**: Inspect every line of code that processes your work
- **Independence**: No vendor lock-in, no surprise pricing changes
- **Customization**: Modify and extend it to fit your exact needs

## What It Does

### AI-Powered Code Assistance
- Generate code from natural language descriptions
- Get context-aware suggestions as you work
- Understand and explain complex codebases
- Support for multiple programming languages

### Built for Developers
- Integrates with your existing workflow
- Works offline when self-hosted
- No telemetry or tracking unless you choose to enable it
- Community-driven features and improvements

## Who It's For

### Solo Developers
Value your privacy and want full control over your development tools.

### Startups & Small Teams
Protect your competitive advantage without paying enterprise AI pricing.

### Enterprises
Meet strict security requirements while providing developers with modern AI tools.

### Open Source Projects
Align your tools with your values—use open source to build open source.

## The Philosophy

We believe powerful AI development tools should be:
- **Transparent**: You should know exactly how your code is processed
- **Private**: Your intellectual property should stay on your infrastructure
- **Accessible**: Available to all developers regardless of budget
- **Community-Driven**: Better through collective contribution, not corporate control
